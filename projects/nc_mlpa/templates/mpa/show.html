Impact Analysis Instructions

This page contains information on potential fishery-specific impacts for a single MPA within a proposal. Potential impacts associated with the MPA are presented as a summary across the entire North Coast Study Region.

Potential impacts are summarized by port and by fishery. Fron north to south, the ports are: Crescent City, Trinidad, Eureka - Arcata, Shelter Cove, Fort Brag, and Albion.

The table output is organized into two columns: the percentage of the total value and the percentage of total area potentially impacted.

A "---" (instead of a number value) means that potential impacts are not available for this fishery. This occurs when 1) the fishery does not exist in the port, 2) insufficient data was collected in that port for that fishery, or 3) fewer than four fishermen were interviewed/participated in that fishery and to maintain confidentiality we are not reporting on that fishery.

PLEASE BE AWARE: Selecting an MPA for analysis may require several minutes to execute. Selecting other buttons within this panel while results are loading will cause unpredictable behavior within this panel. If this is done accidentally, simply close out of the results panel, and then reselect your MPA.

PLEASE NOTE: While the analytical methods are the same, the evaluation framework of MarineMap software varies slightly from the framework used by Ecotrust to prepare the Scientific Advisory Team (SAT) external evaluation. The primary difference is associated with how rounding occurs within each framework. Because of this, there may be minor differences in the values reported. For consistency, potential impacts to arrays should only be compared within the same framework.
